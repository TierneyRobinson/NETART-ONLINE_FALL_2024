Communication Creates Conflict

Heath Bunting, 1995


Bunting strived to use corporate-controlled communication methods to create social connections between regular civilians. In this piece, Bunting made it so users could send messages to one another. Bunting essentially commented on how networks would play a pivotal role in shaping society.


The World In 24 Hours

Robert Adrian, 1982


This piece connected 16 cities worldwide to transmit content over multiple electronic devices. It also highlights the idea of exchange and the social impact the web would have on the world.
