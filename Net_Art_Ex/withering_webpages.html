Epithelia

Mariela Yeregui, 1999


Epithelia consisted of HTML and javascript-driven elements which created a webpage that highlighted and commented on the human body. The website also linked to an online forum where users could leave comments regarding the human body.

Since this work was based on Netscape4.o, Epithelia soon became inaccessible after it was published on the web.


The File Room

Antoni Muntadas : Collaborators, 1994


Muntadas has taken the lost and brought it back to the public. This piece was an installation which contained a database filled with past cases of censorship from around the world. Muntadas' goal was to re-introduce deleted and suppressed material that wouldn't normally be accessible.
